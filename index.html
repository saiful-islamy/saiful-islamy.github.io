Selected Projects

01 · Dissertation · Distinction 75%
Mobile Banking & Women's Financial Inclusion

I wanted to know if a phone could change a woman's financial life. Using World Bank Global Findex 2021 data — 71,000+ women across 144 countries — I built logistic regression, Random Forest, and XGBoost models to find out. The answer was yes, and the numbers tell a human story.

Women with mobile accounts are 2.9x more likely to receive wages directly, +26.2pp more likely to have emergency funds, save +24pp more, and borrow +19.4pp more. XGBoost achieved AUC 0.787. Findings delivered as policy recommendations to stakeholders.
